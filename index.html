WHO ARE WE?

The Mathematics Department of FMKSD ITS is one of the leading Mathematics Departments in Indonesia. The Department’s vision is to be a department with an international reputation in the fields of mathematics and computing and its applications that support science and technology, especially in the fields of industry, energy, marine, finance and information technology that are environmentally friendly.
